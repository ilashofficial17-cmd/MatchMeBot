## Session Summary

Full IT legal compliance consultation and implementation for the MatchMe Telegram dating bot. The session covered legal analysis, document drafting, review, and code integration.

---

## Context & Problem

MatchMeBot is an anonymous Telegram dating bot with:
- Anonymous chat matching (Simple, Flirt, Kink modes)
- 11+ AI chat companions with images/GIFs
- Premium subscriptions via Telegram Stars
- AI-powered moderation
- Partner advertising integrations
- 3 languages (RU/EN/ES)

The project had **no legal documents** — no Terms of Service, no proper Privacy Policy, no age disclaimers, no refund policy. This created legal exposure in US and EU jurisdictions.

---

## Legal Consultation — Key Decisions

### 1. Jurisdiction Focus
- **Primary:** USA (operator lives in US)
- **Secondary:** EU (GDPR, EU AI Act)
- **Excluded:** Russia (operator decided against RF focus due to Telegram regulatory situation in RF)

### 2. Age Verification Approach
- **Decision:** Self-declaration only (no identity verification)
- **Rationale:** Standard practice for anonymous chat platforms. Not a financial service, no KYC requirements.
- **Legal protection:** Users accept full responsibility for providing accurate age. Parents/guardians liable if minor misrepresents age. COPPA disclaimer (no children under 13).

### 3. Refund Policy
- **Decision:** All purchases are final, no refunds
- **Rationale:** Digital content activated immediately. User explicitly waives right of withdrawal.
- **EU compliance:** Covered by Directive 2011/83/EU, Article 16(m) — exception for digital content with immediate activation + explicit consent.
- **Key detail:** Telegram Stars is Telegram's payment system — bot operator doesn't process or store card data. Payment disputes go to Telegram.

### 4. AI Character Images
- **Source:** All AI-generated by operator (Midjourney/similar tools)
- **Characters:** Entirely fictional, not based on real people
- **Legal status:** No copyright issues (AI-generated, no third-party IP). Standard "any resemblance is coincidental" disclaimer added.
- **Important clarification:** Bot does NOT generate images — operator manually uploads all media. AI only generates text.

### 5. Document Format
- **Decision:** Single combined document (Terms of Service & Privacy Policy) per language
- **Rationale:** Telegram bot context — separate multi-page legal docs are overkill. One compact document per language, shown as Telegraph link.
- **Delivery:** Short summary in bot message + link to full Telegraph page. Not files.

### 6. Key Legal Frameworks Referenced
| Framework | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| Section 230 CDA (USA) | Operator not liable for user content |
| COPPA (USA) | Service not for children under 13 |
| GDPR (EU) | Data collection disclosure, user rights, AI decision transparency |
| CCPA (California) | California resident rights |
| EU Directive 2011/83/EU | Digital content refund waiver |
| EU AI Act (Art. 52) | AI disclosure — characters are AI, not real people |
| AAA Arbitration | Dispute resolution mechanism |

## Legal Audit Findings

### Critical Risks Identified
| # | Risk | Severity | Resolution |
|---|------|----------|------------|
| 1 | No age disclaimer for 18+ content | Critical | Added age self-declaration + responsibility clause |
| 2 | No Terms of Service or Privacy Policy | High | Created full legal documents in 3 languages |
| 3 | Data sent to AI APIs without disclosure | High | Added third-party AI processing disclosure |
| 4 | No refund policy | High | Added "all purchases final" clause with EU waiver |
| 5 | No AI disclosure (EU AI Act) | Medium | Added AI character disclaimer |
| 6 | Shadow-ban without notification | Medium | Documented in terms as allowed practice |
| 7 | A/B testing of prices | Medium | Noted; no code change, disclosed in terms |
| 8 | No ad labeling (RF law) | Low | Deprioritized (no RF focus) |

### Items NOT Changed (By Decision)
- No age verification system added (self-declaration sufficient)
- No separate documents for each legal topic (combined into one)
- No RF-specific compliance (ORD registration, RF ad law)

### Architecture

```
Bot Startup:
  main() → create_legal_pages()
    → Telegraph API: createAccount (once)
    → Telegraph API: createPage x3 (RU/EN/ES)
    → Cache URLs to .telegraph_cache.json
    → Subsequent startups load from cache

User Onboarding Flow:
  /start → language selection
    → Show privacy summary + Telegraph link (new!)
    → "Accept" button
    → Registered → main menu
```

### Key Technical Details
- Telegraph pages created via `https://api.telegra.ph/createPage` API
- Access token + page URLs cached in `.telegraph_cache.json` (gitignored)
- `get_legal_url(lang)` returns cached URL for any language
- `disable_web_page_preview=True` on messages with Telegraph links to keep UI clean
- Markdown-like legal text parsed into Telegraph Node format via `_build_legal_content()`
